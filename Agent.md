
# Workspace structure

You are working in a workspace with two projects:

1. caldiy-masterclass - codebase;
2. artefacts - side project, for preparing artifacts fir ai-driven development;

## Artifacts Repo conventions

- Stories live under `artefacts/stories/`.
- Specs live under `artefacts/specs/`
- Plans live under `artefacts/plans/`
- Context maps live under `artefacts/contex-maps/`
- Verification plans live under `artefacts/verification/`
